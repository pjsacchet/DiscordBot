Commands:
  !play:
    Play YouTube link in voice channel [must precede YouTube URL and bot must be connected to voice channel]

  !join:
    Have VibeBot join your voice channel [must be in voice channel prior to command]

  !leave:
    Tell VibeBot to get out of the voice channel

  !pause:
    Pause the song currently playing

  !stop:
    Stop the song currently playing

  !resume:
    Resume the song currently paused

  !updates:
    Prints most recent updates from previous patch

  !help:
    Display this help message
